Help & Support

How do I create a new Circle?

To create a new Circle, go to the 'Create' page, fill out the details about your community, and invite members. Customize settings and start sharing right away!

How do I reset my password?

Visit the 'Settings' page and select 'Change Password'. Follow the instructions to update your password securely.

How can I report inappropriate content?

Use the 'Report' option available on posts or profiles to flag inappropriate content. Our moderation team will review it promptly.

How do I manage my notifications?

Go to the 'Notifications' page to view and manage your alerts. You can mark notifications as read or adjust preferences in 'Settings'.

How do I contact CircleNet support?

You can reach us anytime by filling out the form below. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
